# Workspaces

Workspaces in OpenLabs provide a collaborative environment for teams to work together on lab environments.

## What are Workspaces?

Workspaces are logical containers that:

- Group related templates and lab environments
- Control access for team members
- Organize resources by project, team, or purpose

## Workspace Features

- **Team Collaboration**: Invite team members with different permission levels
- **Resource Organization**: Group related templates and lab environments
- **Shared Access**: Control who can view, edit, or deploy resources
- **Resource Isolation**: Keep projects and teams separate

## Workspace Roles

Workspaces support multiple user roles with different permissions:

- **Owner**: Full control over the workspace and all resources
- **Admin**: Can manage users and resources
- **Member**: Can use resources based on assigned permissions
- **Viewer**: Can only view resources

## Best Practices

- Create separate workspaces for different teams or projects
- Use descriptive names for workspaces
- Regularly review workspace memberships
- Apply the principle of least privilege when assigning roles
